    MEDIA ROOM

    B14

    Located in the very end of B14, there is a media room with actual cinema seets, echo proofing, a 105" projection canvas, and a 5.1 surround sound system. It seats 17 people, but capacity can be expanded to 35 with the foldin chairs from the storage area on the left. You can stream media from your own HDMI compatible devices.
    Remember that the room needs to be booked beforehand! You can place your booking on the sheet outside the room's door.

    Just below building 14, there is a brand new social space with several functions:

    • a community kitchen
    • dining / meeting room
    • a billiard / table tennis room
    • a "hygge" room with sofas an a TV
    • quiet and study areas
    • and a 17 seat media room!

    All residents have access to B14, but certain functions need to be booked in advance (namely the kitchen and the cinema). Booking is possible on the sheets placed outsie the kitchen and the cinema.

    COMMITTEES

    MUSIC ROOM

    In the basement of 18 is a sound dampened music room complete with speakers, amps and microphones, a mixer, an upright piano and a keyboard, and a drumset. Those who have access can sing, practice or play together between 12 and 22 each day. To gain access, please talk to the Office. A deposit of 100 kr. is required. Tingbjerg Kollegiet also has a music committe, which collects those residents who have some hidden musical talents, and (most likely) are users of the music room. If you would like to join the music committee, don't hesitate to write us an e-mail at music@kollegiet.dk, or contact the Office.

    BREWERY ROOM

    There is a brewery right next to the bar, where members of the Brewery committee make all kinds of beers. It is a methodic process that requires patience, but the outcome is quite extraordinary. How many of your friends can say that they have brewed their own beer? If you want to hear more about the brewery committee, please write us an e-mail at brew-crew@kollegiet.dk, or contact the Office.

    GREEN TEAM

    Tingbjerg Kollegiet Green Team is a committee, that organises events at campus, with sustainability and recycling in focus. We have had events such as flea markets, ecological communal eating and are also trying to optimize the sorting of waste in the kitchens. As an example, we are trying to improve the kitchen gardens, while cooperating with the other committees at campus. We would love to have more people help make the Green Team even better! If it sounds like something for you, please write us an e-mail at greenteam@kollegiet.dk, or contact the Office.

    EVENT COMMITTEE

    The event committee are in charge of hosting diffenrent social events at campus. These events could be a Christmas party, halloween etc. If you have any ideas for social events or want to channel your inner partyplanner, please write us an e-mail at activity@kollegiet.dk

    THE COUNCIL

    The Council is a mitigating body between the residents and the Office. It has up to 7 members, and holds meetings between every 2 weeks to a month. They also organize the Residents' meetings every half year, to provide residents with the opportunity to make their voice heard, and also to vote in members for other committees. If you don't like how thing are run, or you think you could make it better, look for the chairman of the Council! Members are always welcome. Send us an e-mail at council@kollegiet.dk.

    SPORT COMMITTEE

    The sport committee are in charge of the gym. We also organizes outside events every once in a while, for example the running club, or football. If you are into moving your body in a good way, make sure to look for us and write an e-mail at sport@kollegiet.dk!
